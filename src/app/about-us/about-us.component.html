Who are we ?


We are a group of undergraduates from the Department of Electrical and Information Engineering, University of Ruhuna. We shared a vision to make it easier for people to buy and sell vegetables online without having the need to go through a middleman. Which will vastly improve the profits of farmers while reducing the cost to buy vegetables.


What is our Goal ?


Our Goal is to satisfy our customers by giving them a better user experience while keeping the product costs at a minimum by eliminating middlemen. Which will allow to maximize the profits of the Farmers.
